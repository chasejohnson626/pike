Policy on Finding

5-7 Finding Time
I believe that you should be able to choose wether or not you want to find from 5-7 AM or PM as it was never specified. Also, if you want to be super diligent you can also choose to find from 5am to 7pm

Finding Method
Of course, our number one finding method is grinding! My way of finding is the only way of finding. I think that it is important that we make ourselves a burden on the members by having at least 2 lesson with each member a week and giving all of them daily calls. I also believe that it is really important that we dot contact because the people that are most likely to be baptized are the ones that are least interested. We need to also call and text each person in our area book at least twice a week. As we diligently pester people their minds will be changed as they realize that we represent Jesus Christ!
