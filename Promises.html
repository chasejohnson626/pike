Removal of MaaS 360

2 Nephi 2:15-16

Wherefore, the Lord God gave unto man that he should act for himself. Wherefore, man could not act for himself save it should be that he was enticed by the one or the other.

I think that by letting us use the gas cards for buying snacks that it fulfills Jesus' promise that we don't need to take thought of what we shall eat but that if we seek first the kingdom of God that these things will be added unto us.
